Validating Web Forms

First name must be and contain 3 - 16 characters

Last name must be and contain 3 - 16 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g. me@mydomain.com

Password must alphanumeric (@, _ and - are also allowed) and be 6 - 20 characters

Telephone must be a valid UK telephone number (11 digits)

Bio must contain only lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens and be 8 - 50 characters
